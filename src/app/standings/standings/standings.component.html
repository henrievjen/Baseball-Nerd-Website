How to Read the Standings

PCT (winning percentage) is wins divided by total games played — it's what determines a division's rank, not raw win total, since teams play an uneven number of games at any given point in the season.

GB (games behind) measures the gap to the leader. It moves in half-game increments: a team one full game behind has one fewer win and one more loss than the leader, while a half-game gap means the two teams have played an uneven number of games.

Since the 2022 postseason expansion, each league sends its three division winners plus three wild card teams to the playoffs — twelve teams total. Wild card seeding is based purely on overall record, regardless of division, and ties for the final spots are broken using head-to-head results first, followed by intra-division and intra-league record.
