What Is Baseball Nerd?

Baseball Nerd is a free, ad-supported web application and Android app built for Major League Baseball fans who want fast, clean access to real-time MLB data — without the clutter of a traditional sports portal. Every feature on the site pulls live data directly from the official MLB Stats API, so scores, standings, player statistics, and team schedules are always current and accurate.

The site covers all 30 MLB franchises across both the American League and National League, from the New York Yankees and Los Angeles Dodgers to the Oakland Athletics and Tampa Bay Rays. Whether you follow one team obsessively or track the whole league, Baseball Nerd gives you a clean, modern interface to do it.

Features

⚾

Live Scores

Follow every game as it happens with live box scores, inning-by-inning run totals, and final results for every MLB game on a given day. Games are organized clearly with pitching matchups, team records, and broadcast information so you always know where to watch.

🏆

Standings

Full AL and NL standings updated daily, including wins, losses, winning percentage, games behind, run differential, home and away splits, and last-10-game records. Wild card standings are tracked separately so you never lose sight of the playoff picture.

📊

Player Stats

Deep-dive career and season statistics for every active MLB player. Batting stats include AVG, OBP, SLG, OPS, home runs, RBIs, stolen bases, and more. Pitching stats cover ERA, WHIP, strikeouts, walks, innings pitched, and win-loss record. Search any player by name to pull up their full profile.

📅

Team Schedules

Full season schedules for all 30 teams, including upcoming games with starting pitcher matchups, past results with final scores, and opponent information. Filter by team to track your favorite club's complete slate of games from Opening Day through the World Series.

🗺️

Depth Charts

Organizational depth charts for every MLB team showing the positional hierarchy from the big-league roster down through the minor league affiliates. See who's starting, who's on the bench, and which prospects are on the rise — useful for fantasy baseball managers and die-hard fans alike.

🌤️

Stadium Weather

Real-time weather conditions at all 30 MLB ballparks. Check temperature, wind speed and direction, humidity, and precipitation chance before any outdoor game. Especially useful for fantasy decisions — wind blowing out at Wrigley Field has historically boosted home run rates significantly.

🔍

Player Search

Search the entire MLB player database by name to instantly pull up career stats, current team, position, batting hand, throwing arm, age, and a player headshot. Covers active major leaguers as well as historical players and minor league prospects in the system.

The Creator

Hi! I'm Henri Evjen, the creator and sole developer of Baseball Nerd. I'm a lifelong baseball fan, avid card collector, and software developer based in the United States. I built this site to solve a simple problem: I wanted a fast, distraction-free way to check scores and stats on my phone without wading through pop-ups, auto-play videos, and endless ads.

Baseball Nerd started as a personal project and grew into something I'm genuinely proud of. Every feature you see was designed and coded by me — from the live score cards to the detailed player profiles. The site runs entirely on the official MLB Stats API (no scraping) and is updated regularly with new features based on feedback from the community.

Outside of Baseball Nerd, I've been collecting baseball cards since I was a kid. I primarily collect vintage cards from the 1950s–1980s as well as modern autograph and relic cards of current players. If you're a fellow collector, check out my eBay store and CollX profile linked below.
