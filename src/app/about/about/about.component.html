The Project

Baseball Nerd is a passion project dedicated to providing baseball fans with clean, fast, and accessible MLB data. From scores and standings to deep-dive player stats and team schedules, the goal is to keep fans informed with a modern, intuitive interface.

The Creator

Hi! I'm Henri Evjen, the creator and developer of Baseball Nerd. I'm an avid baseball fan and collector, and I built this site to merge my love for the game with my passion for software development.
