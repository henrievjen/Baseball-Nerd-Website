What Baseball Nerd Covers

Baseball Nerd is a free web application built for Major League Baseball fans who want fast, no-nonsense access to real-time MLB data. Every statistic, score, and standings update is pulled directly from the official MLB Stats API, ensuring the numbers you see are the same ones MLB uses officially — no estimates, no scraping, no delays.

The site covers all 30 MLB franchises across the American League (AL) and National League (NL), including all six divisions: AL East, AL Central, AL West, NL East, NL Central, and NL West. Whether you're a die-hard Yankees fan, a casual Dodgers follower, or a fantasy baseball manager tracking players across 10 teams, Baseball Nerd is designed to give you what you need quickly and cleanly.

Features at a Glance

⚾

Live Scores

Follow every MLB game in real time with live box scores, inning-by-inning run totals, pitching matchups, and final results. Games are organized by date with easy navigation so you can jump back to yesterday's results or forward to upcoming games.

🏆

Standings

Full American League and National League standings updated daily. View wins, losses, winning percentage, games behind the division leader, run differential, home record, away record, and last-10-game performance. Wild card standings are tracked separately so you never lose sight of the full playoff picture.

📊

Player Stats

Deep-dive batting and pitching statistics for every active MLB player. Batting stats include AVG, OBP, SLG, OPS, home runs, RBIs, stolen bases, strikeouts, and walks. Pitching stats cover ERA, WHIP, strikeouts per nine innings, wins, losses, saves, innings pitched, and opponent batting average.

📅

Team Schedules

Full season schedules for all 30 MLB teams. See upcoming games with confirmed starting pitcher matchups, past results with final scores, and opponent information. Filter by team to track your club's complete slate from Opening Day through the World Series, including the All-Star break and any makeup games.

🗺️

Depth Charts

Organizational depth charts for every MLB team, from the 26-man major league roster down through each minor league affiliate. Essential for fantasy baseball managers who need to know who is starting, who is on the 10-day injured list, and which prospects are being called up or optioned.

🌤️

Stadium Weather

Real-time weather conditions at all 30 MLB ballparks — temperature, wind speed, wind direction, humidity, and chance of precipitation. Wind blowing out at Wrigley Field historically boosts home run rates. Dome stadium conditions are noted, and outdoor park weather updates continuously throughout the day.

🔍

Player Search

Search the complete MLB player database by name. Pull up full player profiles with career stats, current team, position, batting hand, throwing arm, age, height, weight, and a player headshot. Covers active major leaguers, historical players, and prospects in the minor league system.

Where the Data Comes From

All statistics and game data on Baseball Nerd are fetched directly from the MLB Stats API (statsapi.mlb.com), the same official API used by MLB.com and other authorized MLB partners. This means every batting average, ERA, win-loss record, and score you see is sourced from Major League Baseball itself — not estimated, manually entered, or scraped from a secondary source.

The API provides real-time game data during the season, which Baseball Nerd displays with minimal latency. Live scores typically reflect the current game state within seconds of each pitch or play. Historical statistics and season totals are updated after each game concludes and are retroactively corrected if MLB issues official stat changes.

Player headshots, team logos, and other media assets are also served through authorized MLB channels (mlb.com image CDN), so the imagery you see is official and high quality. Baseball Nerd does not host or store player images locally — they are loaded on-demand from MLB's content delivery network.

Baseball Nerd for Fantasy Baseball

Fantasy baseball managers will find Baseball Nerd especially useful during the season. The combination of live scores, real-time standings, detailed player statistics, and organizational depth charts gives you everything you need to make smart roster decisions throughout the week.

Checking the waiver wire? Use the Player Search to look up any player's current stats, position, and team. You can check whether they are currently on the active roster or injured list using the Depth Chart feature, which shows full organizational rosters right down through Triple-A.

Making a start/sit decision? The Stadium Weather tool shows live conditions at every outdoor MLB ballpark so you can factor in wind, temperature, and precipitation into your lineup. Historically, warm temperatures and wind blowing out to the outfield correlate with higher run environments and more home runs — factors that matter when choosing between two similar starting pitchers or hitters.

Tracking a prospect? Depth charts show the entire organizational ladder for each of the 30 teams, letting you monitor when a top prospect gets promoted from Double-A to Triple-A and when they might be on the verge of a major league call-up.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Baseball Nerd free to use?

Yes. Baseball Nerd is completely free for all users. The site is supported by Google AdSense advertisements, which keeps it free while covering hosting, domain, and development costs. If you want to support the project without ads, you can buy the creator a coffee.

How accurate is the data?

All data comes directly from the official MLB Stats API. During live games, scores and play-by-play are updated in near-real time. Season statistics are updated after each game ends and reflect official MLB records.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes — the Baseball Nerd Android app is available on Google Play. Search "Baseball Nerd" in the Play Store, or visit the About page for a direct link. The web app is also fully responsive and works well on any mobile browser.

Does Baseball Nerd require an account?

No registration or account is required. All features — scores, standings, player stats, depth charts, and weather — are available without signing in. Your team preferences and color theme are saved locally in your browser.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Use the Feedback page to send a message directly to the developer. All feedback is read and considered for future updates.
