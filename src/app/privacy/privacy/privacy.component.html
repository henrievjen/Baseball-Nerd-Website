Overview

Baseball Nerd ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Baseball Nerd website and Android application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service. By using Baseball Nerd, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Baseball Nerd does not require account registration. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifying information to use the core features of the site. If you contact us via the Feedback page, we receive only the email address and message you voluntarily submit.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, our hosting provider may collect standard server log data including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited. This information is used solely for site security, performance monitoring, and aggregate analytics. It is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Local Storage

Baseball Nerd stores a small amount of data in your browser's local storage to remember your preferences — specifically your chosen color theme (light or dark mode) and any favorite teams you have selected. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

MLB Stats API

All baseball data displayed on this site is retrieved from the official MLB Stats API (statsapi.mlb.com). Your use of this site may result in requests being made to MLB's servers. Please review MLB's privacy policy at mlb.com for information on how they handle data.

Google AdSense

Baseball Nerd uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other sites on the Internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to Baseball Nerd and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, please visit: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps .

Google Fonts

This site uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. When you visit a page, your browser may make requests to Google's font servers. Please see Google's privacy policy for details.

Buy Me a Coffee

Our support button links to buymeacoffee.com. If you choose to make a contribution through that service, your payment information is handled entirely by Buy Me a Coffee and is subject to their privacy policy and terms of service. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Cookies

Baseball Nerd itself does not set cookies. However, Google AdSense and other third-party services embedded in the site may set cookies on your device. These cookies are governed by the privacy policies of those respective third parties.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on this site and on other websites you visit.

Children's Privacy

Baseball Nerd is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete that information promptly.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information associated with your use of Baseball Nerd. Because the Service does not store user accounts or personal data on our servers, the risk of a data breach affecting your personal information is minimal. All data transmissions between your browser and our servers use HTTPS encryption.
