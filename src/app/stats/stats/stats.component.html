Glossary of Stat Abbreviations

Batting

AVG
Batting average — hits divided by at-bats.
HR
Home runs.
RBI
Runs batted in — runs that score as a direct result of the batter's plate appearance.
OPS
On-base plus slugging — on-base percentage plus slugging percentage, a quick single-number read on overall offense.
Hits
Total hits: singles, doubles, triples, and home runs combined.
SB
Stolen bases.

Pitching

ERA
Earned run average — earned runs allowed per nine innings pitched.
Wins
Decisions credited to the pitcher of record in a team win.
K
Strikeouts.
WHIP
Walks plus hits allowed per inning pitched.
SV
Saves — games finished by a relief pitcher protecting a lead under save-rule conditions.
