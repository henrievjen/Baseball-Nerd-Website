Baseball Nerd — Live MLB Scores, Standings & Stats

Baseball Nerd is a free web application for Major League Baseball fans. Get live scores for every MLB game, full AL and NL standings, player batting and pitching statistics, team schedules, organizational depth charts, and real-time stadium weather — all in one clean, fast interface.

Features

  • Live Scores — Real-time MLB scores for every game with inning-by-inning breakdowns
  • Standings — Full American League and National League standings with wild card tracking
  • Player Stats — Career and season batting and pitching statistics for all MLB players
  • Team Schedules — Full season schedules for all 30 MLB franchises
  • Depth Charts — Organizational depth charts from MLB rosters through the minor leagues
  • Stadium Weather — Live weather conditions at all 30 MLB ballparks
  • Tabletop Baseball — A playable dice baseball simulation using real MLB rosters

Built by Henri Evjen, Baseball Nerd pulls data from the official MLB Stats API and is updated continuously throughout the baseball season. The app covers all 30 teams from the New York Yankees and Los Angeles Dodgers to the Oakland Athletics and Tampa Bay Rays.
