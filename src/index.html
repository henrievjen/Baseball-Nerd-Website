Baseball Nerd — Live MLB Scores, Standings & Stats

Your free, fast MLB companion for all 30 Major League Baseball teams.

Baseball Nerd is a free web application built for Major League Baseball fans who want fast, no-nonsense access to real-time MLB data. Every statistic, score, and standings update is pulled directly from the official MLB Stats API (statsapi.mlb.com) — the same API used by MLB.com. No scraping. No delays. Always accurate.

Features

  • Live Scores — Real-time MLB scores for every game with inning-by-inning breakdowns, pitching matchups, and final results.
  • Standings — Full American League and National League standings including wins, losses, winning percentage, games behind, run differential, and wild card tracking.
  • Player Stats — Career and season batting statistics (AVG, OBP, SLG, OPS, HR, RBI, SB) and pitching statistics (ERA, WHIP, K/9, IP) for all MLB players.
  • Team Schedules — Full season schedules for all 30 MLB franchises with starting pitcher matchups and results.
  • Depth Charts — Organizational depth charts from the major league roster down through all minor league affiliates.
  • Stadium Weather — Live weather conditions at all 30 MLB ballparks including temperature, wind speed, wind direction, and precipitation chance.
  • Player Search — Search the complete MLB player database by name to view full profiles, career stats, and current team information.

All 30 MLB Teams

Baseball Nerd covers every franchise in both leagues and all six divisions:

  • AL East: Baltimore Orioles, Boston Red Sox, New York Yankees, Tampa Bay Rays, Toronto Blue Jays
  • AL Central: Chicago White Sox, Cleveland Guardians, Detroit Tigers, Kansas City Royals, Minnesota Twins
  • AL West: Houston Astros, Los Angeles Angels, Oakland Athletics, Seattle Mariners, Texas Rangers
  • NL East: Atlanta Braves, Miami Marlins, New York Mets, Philadelphia Phillies, Washington Nationals
  • NL Central: Chicago Cubs, Cincinnati Reds, Milwaukee Brewers, Pittsburgh Pirates, St. Louis Cardinals
  • NL West: Arizona Diamondbacks, Colorado Rockies, Los Angeles Dodgers, San Diego Padres, San Francisco Giants

About the Data

All data on Baseball Nerd is sourced from the official MLB Stats API. Live game data is updated in near-real time during the season. Season statistics are finalized after each game and retroactively corrected if MLB issues official stat adjustments. Player headshots and team logos are loaded from MLB's official content delivery network.

About Baseball Nerd

Baseball Nerd was built by Henri Evjen, a lifelong baseball fan and software developer. The site is completely free to use and is supported by Google AdSense advertisements. An Android app is also available on the Google Play Store. Feedback and feature requests can be submitted through the Feedback page.
